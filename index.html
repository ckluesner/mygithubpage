About

Steak With A Story is an up and coming platform that is intended to provide a face for the hardworking farmers and ranchers behind an industry that might seem to be dominated by factory farms and corporate companies.


All of these featured stories are true, and all of these farmers and ranchers are close personal friends who have helped me along my journey to become a first generation farmer.
